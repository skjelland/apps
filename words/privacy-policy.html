Privacy Policy

App: Word Finder
Last updated: 29 May 2026

Data sent to our server

When you perform a word search, the app sends your search input (letters and any word pattern constraints) and your selected language to our server at skjelland.net for processing. The server returns matching words and does not store, log, or retain your search queries. All communication is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

As with any HTTPS connection, standard technical data such as your IP address and device platform information is transmitted as part of the request. This data is not stored or used to identify you.

No personal data collected

The app does not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

No analytics or tracking

The app does not use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

No account required

The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Local storage

The app may save your preferences (such as selected language or tile set) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.
